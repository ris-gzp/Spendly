Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly: your name, email address, and the expense data you enter (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions). We may also collect basic usage data such as login timestamps to maintain your account security.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expenses, generating spending summaries, and authenticating your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorised systems required to operate the service. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use essential third-party services (such as hosting providers) to operate the platform, and these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations. We do not integrate with any advertising or analytics networks.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact information provided on the Spendly platform. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly.
